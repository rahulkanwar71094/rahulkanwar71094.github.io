About


is an online, curated and narrative based Archive, which traces the histroy of India & its subcontinent.

Moreover, it's an archive-cum-weekly newsletter which shares stories/ articles/ pictures/ links about Indian culture, religion, food, people, politics, languages, geography, music, science (& much more) from the past.

Very often Histroy is misinterpreted, it is considered to be just about dates and quotes and obscure provisos.

We believe history is about life. About change. About consequences. Cause and effect. It’s about the mystery of human nature. The mystery of time. And it isn’t just about politics and the military and social issues, which is almost always the way it’s taught.

It’s about music and poetry and drama and science and medicine and money and love. In fact it could be about anything and everything.

Every possible thing on this planet has it's own histroy, which needs to be told, as it reveals valuable information about people, families & ancestors, cultures, lifestyles, traditions, choices, circumstances and thereby consequences of those times.

That's exactly what we do! We won't share dates & quotes. We will share the experiences.

With India Archive, we intend to tell Indian histroy through stories, one at a time. Join Us.
