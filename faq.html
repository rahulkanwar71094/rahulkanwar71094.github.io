Q. What is India Archive?
A. The India Archive is an project to share and archive stories about the histroy of India and its subcontinents.
We share stories about culture, religion, food, people, politics, languages, geography from the past.

Q. Why do we need India Archive?
A. History is not about dates and quotes and obscure provisos. History is about life. About change. About consequences. Cause and effect. It’s about the mystery of human nature. The mystery of time.

And it isn’t just about politics and the military and social issues, which is almost always the way it’s taught. It’s about music and poetry and drama and science and medicine and money and love.

We feel histroy is the most romanatic subject, if taught properly.

This project traces the histroy of India in an very conventional manner that will make you think no matter how much you know about India.

Q. Who started this project & who funds it?
